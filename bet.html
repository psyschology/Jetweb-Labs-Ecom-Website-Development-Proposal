Proposal: Full-Featured Betting Website MVP

A strategic plan to launch, test, and secure funding for your platform.

Date: 12 September 2025 | Budget: ₹1,30,000 INR (Fixed)

Executive Summary

We will deliver a production-ready MVP (Minimum Viable Product) of a full betting website experience, optimized for web and mobile. The deliverable will allow users to register, login, deposit (via configured gateway), place bets on sports/games using dummy or static odds, view betting history, request withdrawals, and includes an admin panel to manage users and bets.

Why an MVP-First Approach is Smart

Full production betting platforms require expensive licensing, third-party live odds feeds, and lengthy payment processor approvals. With this MVP, you get a tangible, working product to test business flows, gather user feedback, and demonstrate traction to secure funding or merchant accounts for the full-scale launch.

MVP Scope (Included in ₹1.3L)

User-Facing Features

  • User Registration & Login: Secure email + password authentication, with optional OTP.
  • Profile & KYC Placeholder: Profile page with a form to request KYC (full verification service is out of scope).
  • Wallet (Internal Ledger): Credit (deposit request), debit (place bet), and balance view.
  • Deposit & Withdraw Flows: Admin-approved request flows for deposits and withdrawals.
  • Betting UI: Sports/games listing, match details, and a betting slip for placing bets (using static/manual odds for the MVP).
  • History & Receipts: Complete user bet history and individual bet receipts.
  • Legal Pages: Templates for Responsible Gaming, Terms & Conditions, and Privacy Policy.
  • Basic Email Notifications: For account creation and deposit request confirmation.

Admin / Operator Panel

  • User Management: View, suspend, and manage KYC status for all users.
  • Wallet Adjustments: Admin override for manual credit/debit adjustments.
  • Event Management: Create and manage events & matches with manual odds entry for the MVP.
  • Bet Management: View all bets, perform manual settlement, and export reports to CSV.
  • Transaction Management: Approve or reject deposit and withdrawal requests.

Platform Basics & Quality

  • Responsive UI: Seamless experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
  • Basic Security Hardening: HTTPS, input validation, and bcrypt password hashing.
  • Deployment Ready: Comes with deployment scripts and is Docker-ready.
  • Documentation & Training: Includes handover docs and a 1-hour training session.

Out of Scope (Not in this Budget)

To maintain budget and timeline, these are excluded from the MVP:

  • Live Odds Feed APIs: Integration with services like Sportradar (these are separate, licensed products).
  • Merchant Onboarding/Fees: Client is responsible for payment gateway fees and securing a compliant merchant account.
  • Automated KYC/ID Verification: Services like Onfido or Sumsub can be integrated as a future add-on.
  • Advanced Infrastructure: Complex anti-fraud systems or high-frequency real-time scaling.
  • Native Mobile Apps: We deliver a high-quality responsive web application.
  • Legal & Licensing Costs: Client is responsible for all legal compliance, licensing, and taxes.
