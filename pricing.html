Executive Summary & Market Positioning

Our Core Pricing Strategy

Our strategy is to aggressively penetrate the Indian SME market by positioning Jetweb Labs as the premier provider of **high-value, low-cost digital solutions**. We will deliberately undercut the bloated pricing of established agencies while offering a significantly more professional and scalable product than low-end freelancers. Our profitability will be driven by systemized delivery, lean operations, and scaling volume.

Market Research Findings:

  • Freelancers (India): ₹5,000 - ₹20,000 for a basic site. Quality is inconsistent, support is unreliable.
  • Small Agencies (Tier 2/3 cities): ₹25,000 - ₹75,000. Often use outdated tech and templates.
  • Mid-Size Agencies (Metros): ₹80,000 - ₹3,00,000+. Professional, but too expensive for our target SMEs.

Our Strategic Advantage:

  • Price Disruption: Our `JetLite` and `JetLaunch` packages are priced to be irresistible to the freelancer market.
  • Technology Superiority: We offer modern tech (Next.js, Headless CMS) at prices where others offer basic WordPress.
  • Scalable Model: By systemizing our process (CTO's boilerplate), we can maintain healthy margins even at lower price points.
