Understanding Recurring Costs

It's important to distinguish our one-time development fee from the ongoing costs of running your store. These fees are paid directly to the service providers.

Shopify Subscription

This is the monthly or annual fee for using the Shopify platform. The "Basic" plan is often a great starting point.

~₹1,999 / month

Payment Gateway Fees

Payment gateways (like Razorpay) charge a small percentage per transaction they process for you.

~2% + GST per transaction
